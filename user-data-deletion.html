User Data Deletion Policy

Types of Data Collected

We collect the following types of data:

  • User profile information (name, email address, etc.)
  • Posts and comments made by the user
  • Any additional information you provide to us directly

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log into your account.
  2. Navigate to the account settings page.
  3. Click on the “Delete Account” button.
  4. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account and data.

Data Deletion Process

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  • Verify your identity to prevent unauthorized deletions.
  • Permanently delete all your data from our systems within 30 days.
  • Inform you via email once your data has been successfully deleted.
